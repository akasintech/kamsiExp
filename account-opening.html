Open Your Account



Welcome to KamsiExpress! Opening an account with us is quick and easy. Once you have an account, you can track your shipments, access exclusive rates, and manage all your shipping needs in one place.


Benefits of Having an Account:

  • Track all your shipments in real-time
  • Access exclusive shipping rates and discounts
  • Manage multiple shipments from one dashboard
  • Receive email notifications about shipment status
  • View shipping history and invoices
  • Faster checkout process for repeat shipments

How to Open an Account:

To open an account with KamsiExpress, please contact our customer service team:

Email: info@kamsiexpress.com

Phone: +1 908 275 3675, +1 848 628 0207

Office Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM EST


Our team will guide you through the account opening process and provide you with your account credentials. You can also visit our office locations to open an account in person.


Required Information:

  • Full name or company name
  • Valid email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Government-issued ID (for verification)
  • Business registration documents (for business accounts)

Once your account is approved, you'll receive an email with your login credentials and instructions on how to access your account dashboard.
