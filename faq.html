Frequently Asked Questions

Home FAQ
How long does shipping take from USA to other countries?

Air freight shipments typically take 4-6 days for delivery, while ocean freight shipments take 4-6 weeks. Delivery times may vary based on destination, customs clearance, and service level selected.

What is the free storage period for shipments?

KamsiExpress provides free storage for seven calendar days at our warehouses. After this period, storage charges will apply based on the duration of storage.

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment using the tracking number provided when your shipment is processed. Visit our Track Shipment page and enter your tracking number for real-time updates.

What items are prohibited from shipping?

Prohibited items include hazardous materials, illegal substances, weapons, cash, perishable foods without proper refrigeration, and items restricted by destination country regulations. Please contact us for a complete list.

Do you offer insurance for shipments?

Yes, we offer optional cargo insurance for your shipments. Insurance can be purchased when authorizing your shipment and is recommended for valuable items.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, bank transfers, credit cards, and other approved payment methods. Payment terms can be discussed during shipment authorization.

Can I consolidate multiple shipments?

Yes, KamsiExpress offers consolidation services to combine multiple shipments, which helps reduce shipping costs. Packages can be stored at our warehouse and shipped together.

Do you provide packaging materials?

Yes, we provide standard packing and shipping materials at no cost. Additional protective materials are available at cost if needed for fragile or valuable items.

What happens if my shipment is delayed?

If your shipment experiences delays, we will notify you immediately. Delays can occur due to customs clearance, weather conditions, or carrier schedules. We work diligently to minimize delays and keep you informed throughout the process.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes, KamsiExpress handles customs clearance for shipments. Our experienced customs agents expedite the clearance process while minimizing charges where possible. Additional customs duties and taxes may apply based on destination country regulations.
