Ocean Freight Authorization



KamsiExpress provides comprehensive ocean freight services from the United States to Africa and beyond. To authorize your ocean freight shipment, please follow the guidelines below.


Authorization Requirements:

  • Complete shipment authorization form
  • Valid identification document
  • Commercial invoice (for commercial shipments)
  • Packing list with detailed item descriptions
  • Proof of ownership or purchase documentation
  • Customs declaration forms (if required)
  • Payment authorization or proof of payment

Authorization Process:

  1. Submit your shipment details and required documents
  2. Our team will review and verify all documentation
  3. You'll receive a quote for shipping costs and services
  4. Approve the quote and authorize the shipment
  5. Make payment as per agreed terms
  6. Receive confirmation and tracking information
  7. Your shipment will be scheduled for the next available vessel

Service Options:

Door to Door

Complete service from pickup to final delivery at your specified address.

Door to Port

Pickup from your location and delivery to destination port.

Consolidation

Combine multiple shipments for reduced shipping costs.


Important Notes:

  • Ocean freight shipments typically take 4-6 weeks for delivery
  • All shipments are subject to customs clearance at destination
  • Additional charges may apply for customs duties and taxes
  • Insurance is recommended for valuable shipments
  • Container loading schedules are flexible to accommodate customers
  • Swift customs clearance services available
