Package Acceptance



KamsiExpress accepts packages at our warehouse locations for consolidation and shipping. We provide flexible package acceptance services to accommodate your shipping needs.


Package Acceptance Guidelines:

  • All packages must be properly labeled with recipient information
  • Packages should be securely packaged to prevent damage during transit
  • Prohibited items are not accepted (see list below)
  • Packages are accepted during business hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM EST
  • Large shipments may require prior notification
  • All packages are subject to inspection for security and customs compliance

Prohibited Items:

The following items cannot be shipped through KamsiExpress:

  • Hazardous materials (flammable, explosive, toxic substances)
  • Perishable food items without proper refrigeration
  • Illegal substances or contraband
  • Weapons, ammunition, or explosives
  • Cash, currency, or negotiable instruments
  • Precious metals or stones without proper documentation
  • Live animals (except with special arrangements)
  • Items restricted by destination country regulations

Package Acceptance Process:

  1. Deliver your package to our warehouse location or schedule a pickup
  2. Provide shipping information and recipient details
  3. Our staff will inspect and verify the package contents
  4. You'll receive a tracking number for your shipment
  5. Package will be consolidated with other shipments for cost efficiency
  6. Shipment will be dispatched according to your selected service level
